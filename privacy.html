Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Locaro ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our iOS app.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in with Google or Apple.
  • Location data: precise location to show nearby restaurants on the map. We only access location while you use the app.
  • User content: posts, reviews, photos, and guides you create within the app.
  • Usage data: interactions such as places visited, wishlisted, and posts viewed — used to personalise your feed.
  • Device data: crash reports and performance data collected via Firebase Crashlytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Locaro service.
  • To show you relevant restaurants near your location.
  • To enable social features: following friends, sharing guides, activity notifications.
  • To detect and prevent abuse or fraudulent activity.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Firebase (Google): authentication, database, storage, and crash reporting.
  • Google Maps & Places API: to display maps and restaurant information.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your profile, posts, and guides from our servers.

Children's Privacy

Locaro is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
